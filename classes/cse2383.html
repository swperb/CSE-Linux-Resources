CSE 2383: Data Structures and Algorithms

Data Structures and Algorithms is a course with the sole intention of introducing students to data structures, how they operate, how they are used, and how they are implemented. Throughout this course, you will be tested on your ability to build and understand structures of computer memory used in every software system. Using the C++ language, you will be tasked with implementing key data structures by enforcing and building upon object-oriented principles, as well as use previously introduced algorithms to manipulate this data.

About

As the creator of this website, my vision was to enhance the learning experience for CSE students here at MSU. The goal was and still is to fill in the gaps left from classes due to strict college schedules and offer supplemental material to enhance students' performance, not only in the classroom, but in personal programming experiences as well.
