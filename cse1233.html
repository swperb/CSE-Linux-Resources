CSE 1233: Programming in C

Programming in C is a course offered within the CSE department for CSE majors and non-majors alike, focusing on providing a conveinent way to introduce low-level programming concepts to majors that do not heavily rely on programming, but moreso benefit from it. These majors can include Business, Math, Statistics, etc.
